Air Quality Prediction in Monterrey

This project focuses on predicting air pollutant levels in Monterrey, using Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) neural networks. By analyzing historical data from 2022 to 2024 and incorporating meteorological variables, temporal patterns, and feature engineering, the model aims to provide reliable forecasts for pollutants like PM10, SO2, CO, and NOX. The goal is to support proactive air quality management and mitigate health risks associated with pollution.
